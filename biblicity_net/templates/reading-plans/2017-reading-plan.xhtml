2017 Reading Plan

The Bible Overview Reading Plan provides an overview of the Bible in 52 weeks, reading about a chapter a day, six days a week. You won’t read every story or every important passage, but you will get a good overview of what the Bible contains.

Every Sunday there will also be an optional reading of another passage.
